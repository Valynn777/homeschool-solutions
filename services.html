Custom Curriculum Design

Faith-based, professionally designed curriculum materials created specifically for your family using evidence-based instructional design methods. Due to the intensive nature of custom curriculum design, I work with one family at a time on these projects.

All curriculum is designed using professional instructional design methods, incorporating AI-assisted tools under careful oversight and customization to ensure high-quality, faith-based learning materials tailored specifically to your family.

All materials are delivered digitally, allowing your child to work online, offline on your computer, or print materials as needed — giving you flexibility and convenience without sacrificing educational quality. Want printed copies? I can order them for you — pay the printing cost plus a $20+ handling fee (depending on complexity of order).
