Sliding Scale Pricing

Making homeschool support accessible to all families

Our Commitment to Accessibility

We believe that every family deserves access to quality homeschool support, regardless of financial circumstances. Our sliding scale pricing program offers reduced rates based on household income, ensuring that financial constraints don't prevent you from receiving the guidance and encouragement you need.

All applications are reviewed confidentially and with compassion. We understand that every family's situation is unique.

Application Process

  1. Complete the application form below with your household income information
  2. Upload proof of income (recent pay stub, tax return, benefit statement, or other documentation)
  3. Your application will be reviewed confidentially within 7-14 business days
  4. You'll receive an email with your approved rate and next steps
  5. Once approved, you can schedule your parent support session at your adjusted rate

Please note: In some cases, we may be able to review applications sooner than 14 days. We appreciate your patience as we carefully review each application.

What Happens After You Apply?

  1. You'll receive an immediate email confirmation that we've received your application
  2. Your information will be reviewed confidentially by our team
  3. Within 7-14 business days (possibly sooner), you'll receive an email with:
    • Your approved sliding scale rate
    • Instructions for scheduling your first session
    • Payment information at your adjusted rate
  4. Once approved, you can begin scheduling services immediately

Questions? If you have any questions about the sliding scale program or application process, please don't hesitate to contact us.
