Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Faithful Way Homeschool Solutions ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://fwhomeschoolsolutions.dev/ and use our services.

By using our website and services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide

When you use our services, schedule appointments, or complete forms, we may collect:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address
  • Parent Support Session Information: Details about your homeschool situation, children's ages/grades, curriculum preferences, educational goals, and challenges
  • Payment Information: Processed securely through third-party payment processors (Stripe, PayPal, Square) - we do not store complete payment card details on our servers
  • Communications: Messages sent through contact forms, emails, or during support sessions

Information Automatically Collected

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, links clicked, browser type, device information
  • Log Data: IP address, access times, referring website addresses
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance your experience (see Cookies section below)

Free Tools Usage Data

Important: Most of our free tools (transcript generator, schedule builder, lesson generator, worksheet generator) operate entirely within your browser using client-side JavaScript. Data you enter into these tools is:

  • Processed locally on your device
  • NOT transmitted to or stored on our servers
  • NOT accessible to us unless you explicitly share it
  • Stored only in your browser's local storage (which you can clear at any time)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide Services: Deliver parent support sessions, create custom curriculum, and fulfill service requests
  • Communication: Respond to inquiries, send appointment confirmations, provide session summaries and follow-up materials
  • Payment Processing: Process payments for services through secure third-party processors
  • Improve Our Services: Analyze usage patterns to enhance website functionality and service quality
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Marketing: Send occasional updates about services (you can opt-out at any time)

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to:

With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you explicitly consent, such as when requesting us to coordinate with other educational service providers.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help websites remember your preferences and improve functionality.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for basic website functionality and security
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • Functionality Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services like Calendly for appointment scheduling

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect website functionality. Most browsers allow you to:

  • View and delete cookies
  • Block third-party cookies
  • Block all cookies (may impact site functionality)
  • Clear cookies when closing your browser

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information, including:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Secure server infrastructure
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security assessments
  • Secure payment processing through PCI-compliant providers

Important: While we strive to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of your data.

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to:

  • Provide services you've requested
  • Comply with legal obligations (e.g., tax records, payment records)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements
  • Maintain business records for legitimate purposes

Client Records: Information from parent support sessions is typically retained for 3-5 years after the last service date for continuity of care and legal compliance.

Payment Records: Financial transaction records are retained for 7 years for tax and legal purposes.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.
