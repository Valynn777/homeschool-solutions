Custom Curriculum Design

With 14 years of homeschooling experience and professional training in Curriculum Development and Learning Theory, I create faith-based, educationally sound materials using proven instructional design methods. Whether you need focused unit studies, comprehensive digital textbooks, or interactive lessons, each project is designed specifically for your family's unique needs and learning goals.

All materials are created collaboratively with parents, incorporating your insights and objectives to ensure lessons resonate with your student's needs, interests, and learning style. Browse the samples below to see the quality and variety of curriculum options available.

Note: Interactive lessons and tools are best viewed and used on larger screens (tablets, laptops, or desktops) for the optimal learning experience.

Curriculum Options

  • Unit Studies: Focused 10-30 page studies on specific topics, perfect for in-depth exploration without overwhelming. Available in printable or digital format.
  • Digital Textbooks: Comprehensive course materials from elementary through high school level (50-300+ pages), professionally structured and aligned with learning standards.
  • Interactive Studies: Digital-only lessons with interactive features such as drag-and-drop activities, instant feedback, simulations, and multimedia content for engaging, self-guided learning.
  • PWA Learning Hub: Custom progressive web app that organizes all your student's lessons, tools, and resources in one accessible location—works offline and on any device.
