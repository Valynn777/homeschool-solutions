About Faithful Way

Supporting homeschool families with experience and heart

Our Mission

At Faithful Way Homeschool Solutions, we believe that every family's educational journey is unique and precious. Our mission is to provide practical support, encouragement, and tools that empower homeschool families to thrive.

We're here to help you navigate the joys and challenges of homeschooling with confidence, offering guidance rooted in faith and real-world experience.

Real Experience, Real Support

With 14 years of hands-on homeschooling experience, I've walked the same path you're on. I understand what it's like to wonder if you're doing enough, to feel overwhelmed by curriculum choices, and to celebrate those precious breakthrough moments when everything finally clicks for your child.

My educational background includes university coursework in Social Sciences, including child development, which has given me valuable insight into how children learn and grow. But honestly, my greatest education has come from 14 years of researching, evaluating, and adapting countless curricula in search of materials that truly fit our family's needs and values.

Like many homeschool parents, I spent years tweaking ready-made curricula, piecing together resources from different sources, and adapting lessons to match our learning style and faith perspective. When I couldn't find exactly what we needed—especially faith-aligned, Torah-observant materials with a young-earth creation worldview—I realized I had the experience and passion to create it myself. I've dedicated myself to formal curriculum design, bringing together everything I've learned from years of trial and error, research, and real-world homeschooling.

I combine hands-on experience with ongoing professional training in Curriculum Development and Learning Theory to ensure lessons are biblically sound and educationally effective.

My curriculum is developed using professional Instructional Design methodologies, including:

  • Evidence-Based Strategies: Lessons designed to maximize retention and engagement.
  • The ADDIE Model: A professional framework (Analysis, Design, Development, Implementation, Evaluation) used to create structured, logical lesson flows.
  • Bloom's Taxonomy: Ensuring lessons go beyond simple memorization to encourage critical thinking and deep understanding.

This journey has taught me what works, what doesn't, and—most importantly—that there's no one-size-fits-all solution. I've learned to recognize different learning styles, identify gaps in commercial curricula for specific needs, and create materials that are both educationally sound and practical for busy homeschool families.

I'm passionate about homeschooling and genuinely love helping families find their own unique rhythm. Whether you're brand new to homeschooling or looking for a fresh approach after years on this journey, I'm here to offer practical guidance, honest insights from real experience, and the kind of encouragement that only comes from walking this path myself.

My Approach to Curriculum Design

Creating high-quality, faith-based curriculum requires both educational expertise and modern tools. I leverage advanced AI technology as part of my professional design process, which I carefully oversee, customize, and review to ensure every lesson meets rigorous educational standards and aligns with your family's faith-based values.

This approach allows me to combine the efficiency of modern technology with my 14 years of homeschooling experience and professional training in curriculum development. Each lesson is thoughtfully designed, thoroughly reviewed for accuracy and biblical alignment, and customized to your family's specific needs and learning goals.

The result is comprehensive, professionally-structured materials that maintain the personal touch and educational rigor you expect, while being created efficiently enough to offer fair pricing to homeschool families.

How I Can Help

  • Getting Started: Help you understand your state's homeschool laws and requirements so you can begin with confidence.
  • Finding Curriculum: Navigate the overwhelming world of curriculum choices and find what fits both your state requirements and your child's learning needs. I can help you select from established publishers or create custom materials tailored to your family.
  • Custom Curriculum Design: Create faith-based, educationally sound curriculum materials using professional instructional design methods—from focused unit studies to comprehensive digital textbooks.
  • Simple Planning: Create a realistic daily rhythm and schedule that works for your unique family.
  • Ongoing Support: Answer questions, troubleshoot challenges, and provide encouragement along the way.

Important Note: All curriculum design work is done based solely on information and input you provide as the parent. I do not work directly with or provide tutoring/coaching services to students.
