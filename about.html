About Faithful Way

Supporting homeschool families with experience and heart

Our Mission

At Faithful Way Homeschool Solutions, we believe that every family's educational journey is unique and precious. Our mission is to provide practical support, encouragement, and tools that empower homeschool families to thrive.

We're here to help you navigate the joys and challenges of homeschooling with confidence, offering guidance rooted in faith and real-world experience.

Real Experience, Real Support

With 14 years of hands-on homeschooling experience and a university background in the Social Sciences, I offer a unique blend of academic insight and practical application. My goal is to help you navigate the sea of options to find the specific resources and rhythms that work for your family while staying Torah-aligned.

My educational background includes university coursework in Social Sciences, including child development, which has given me valuable insight into how children learn and grow. But honestly, my greatest education has come from 14 years of researching, evaluating, and adapting countless curricula in search of materials that truly fit our family's needs and values.

I provide personalized support in narrowing down curriculum choices and ensuring they align with your state standards. While I am not a legal expert, I can help you understand your local requirements and point you toward the right organizations for filing. To ensure you have a clear path forward, I provide a detailed summary of our support session along with any custom action plans we develop together.

My heart is to provide the direction and encouragement you need to homeschool with confidence and honor the Messiah in your home.

My Approach to Instructional Support

I combine practical experience with modern tools and professional training to provide efficient, faith-aligned support for families. My focus is on delivering clear guidance, reliable resources, and actionable plans that fit your child's learning style and your family's values.

How I Can Help

  • Getting Started: Help you understand your state's homeschool laws and requirements so you can begin with confidence.
  • Finding Resources: Navigate the overwhelming world of materials and publishers to find options that fit your state's requirements and your child's learning needs.
  • Materials Guidance: Receive practical recommendations and planning help to implement chosen resources effectively.
  • Simple Planning: Create a realistic daily rhythm and schedule that works for your unique family.
  • Ongoing Support: Answer questions, troubleshoot challenges, and provide encouragement along the way.

Important Note: All planning and materials guidance is provided based on information and input you provide as the parent. I do not work directly with or provide tutoring/coaching services to students.
